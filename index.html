About Me

Graduate student and hands-on software developer who loves turning ideas into playable, testable, buildable things. Primarily working with C++ and Unreal Engine 5, I’m also constantly exploring new frameworks: from crafting retro-inspired games in PICO-8 to experimenting with Godot and prototyping roguelikes with low-level frameworks. My daily work is fueled by curiosity and the drive to learn through doing, whether that means building a text-based game engine from scratch or trying out a completely new technology. I’m passionate about creating engaging experiences, solving complex problems, and collaborating with others who share a love for code and creativity.
